Stop letting refund cases disappear into “we’ll get back to you.”

RefundRadar turns a refund, cancellation, warranty, or vendor dispute into a practical follow-up loop: what to send, when to send it, what proof to keep, and when to escalate.

New in this build: save multiple cases locally, export a Markdown case packet, print records, and download a calendar reminder for the next follow-up.
